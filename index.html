About Us

About Us

Welcome to Quiz@JSR, your premier destination for professional quiz organizing services. With over a decade of experience in the industry, we have been proudly serving our clients since our establishment in 2014. At Quiz@JSR, we are passionate about the art of quizzing and dedicated to delivering top-notch events that engage, entertain, and educate participants of all ages.

  • Our Journey: Founded in 2014 by a team of enthusiastic quizzers, Quiz@JSR quickly rose to prominence in the quiz organizing landscape. Over the years, we have honed our craft, refined our techniques, and expanded our reach to become a trusted name in the industry. Our journey has been characterized by a relentless pursuit of excellence and a commitment to delivering memorable quiz experiences.
